(OOS) Out of Stock Dashboard

The costly ramifications of out of stocks continue to plague the industry.

According to the FMI/GMA Trading Partner Alliance Report:

  • The OOS rate on regularly priced items averages 8%.

  • The OOS rate on promoted items averages between 13% and 15%.

  • The OOS problem costs the grocery industry approximately 4% in lost sales every year.

GlobalWorx now gives you the visibility you never had before with real-time out of stock transparency.

OOSAlert provides the tools you need in order to solve the industry-wide out of stock problem. This advanced notification system helps you and your trading partners locate, notify, and correct costly out-of-stocks and other merchandising issues in hours instead of days.

Learn more about how you can use search filters to drill down on your out of stock data.

Filters:

Aggregation filter

Default is Daily (can be customized). Works in conjunction with the Daterange filter to populate the Trendline chart. For example, select "Weekly" aggregation with "30 days" for daterange filter and the Trendline chart will display the weeks of the month for that daterange.

Daterange filter

Default is 7 days (can be customized). User can select from pre-populated date ranges, or enter a custom from/to date.

Vendor filter

Suppliers with multiple Distributors will have a Vendor filter. All vendors are displayed by default (can be customized). User has the ability to select 1 or more vendors from this filter.

Region filter

Default is all Regions (can be customzed). Filters the entire dashboard by the selected Region. User has the ability to select 1 or more regions from this filter.

District filter

Default is all districts (can be customized). Filter is populated when 1 or more Regions are selected from the Region filter. User has the ability to select 1 or more districts from this filter. Filters the entire dashboard by the selected district.

(CSI) Critical Service Issue Dashboard

Detection. Action. Results.

Metric Definitions

CSI Past Due:

The metric is tracking the time between when the supplier states they expect to resolve the service issue and when they press the complete button. If the supplier does not complete the service issue in the time frame they stated, then it is marked as past due.

CSI Color Scale:

Green: Less than or equal to 5%

Yellow: Above 5% and less than 15%

Red: Above 15%

CSI Unresponsive:

All service issues that went through all protocol contacts for that supplier at that store and did not provide a response to when it will be resolved.

CSI Color Scale:

Green: Less than or equal to 5%

Yellow: Above 5% and less than 15%

Red: Above 15%

CSI Accepted:

The aggregated total amount of service issues that were accepted by the supplier. Service issues can be accepted at any time whether it has elapsed the escalation time period of 1 hour and 30 minutes.

CSI Color Scale:

Green: Greater than or equal to 95%

Yellow: Above 85% and less than 95%

Red: Below 85%

CSI Outstanding:

The aggregated total of all service issues that have not been verified. This metric is the total number of service issues that have not been verified compared to the total.

CSI Color Scale:

Green: Less than or equal to 5%

Yellow: Above 5% and less than 15%

Red: Above 15%

CSI Unsatisfactory:

The aggregated total of all service issues that were verified and marked as being unsatisfactory. The total amount as seen in the dashboard is determined from the total amount of service issues that were verified. This means it will only show the total for verified service issues and not total amount submitted.

CSI Color Scale:

Green: Less than or equal to 5%

Yellow: Above 5% and less than 15%

Red: Above 15%

    Scorecard Report

    Measuring key performance indicators to track in-store service levels.

    The scorecard measures 4 performance indicators:

  • Average CSIs per store
  • Unrespnsive CSIs
  • Average scanned OOS per store
  • Average lost sales per store

    • Scorecard color scale

    Less than or equal to 0%

    Greater than 5%

    Less than or equal to 5%

    Scorecards can be created for any stakeholder including:

  • Manufacturers/Distributors
  • Category Management
  • Regional Management
  • District Management
  • Store Management

    • Learn more about how you can download scorecard so you can supply information to all stakeholders

    Filters:

    Scorecard period filter

    User has the ability to select from 30 days up to 1 year. If the user selects 30 days, then the score will be calculated based on performance of the last 30 days to the prior 30 days. An example of the 30 day calculation would be comparing September against August.
